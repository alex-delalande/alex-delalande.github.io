I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in applied mathematics advised by Quentin Mérigot and Frédéric Chazal. I work on optimal transport and some of its applications in statistics and machine learning.

Prior to that, I graduated from École Centrale Paris in Applied Mathematics and got a M.Sc. in Machine Learning from ENS Paris-Saclay.
