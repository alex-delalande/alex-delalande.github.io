I am a postdoctoral researcher at EPFL, hosted by the DOLA chair led by Lénaïc Chizat.

Before that, I was a postdoc at the Lagrange Center where I was hosted by the optimal transport team. I did a Ph.D. in applied mathematics at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d'Orsay (Université Paris-Saclay) and within the INRIA DataShape team. My advisors were Quentin Mérigot and Frédéric Chazal.

My research interests revolve around the theory and the applications of the optimal transport problem.
