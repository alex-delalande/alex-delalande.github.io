In 2024, I was a postdoctoral researcher at EPFL, hosted by the DOLA chair led by Lénaïc Chizat. Before that, in 2023, I held a postdoctoral position at the Lagrange Center, where I was hosted by the optimal transport team. From 2019 to 2022, I pursued a Ph.D. in applied mathematics at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d'Orsay (Université Paris-Saclay) and was affiliated with the INRIA DataShape team. My advisors were Quentin Mérigot and Frédéric Chazal.

My research interests lie in the theory and applications of the optimal transport problem.
